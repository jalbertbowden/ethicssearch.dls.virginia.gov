Identify the subject matter (with as much specificity as possible) for which you expect to lobby
  1. 3.The veterans of Foreign Wars (VFW) is concerned with two main issues: those affecting veterans, the military, and their families living in Virginia either during or after military service and those issues related to charitable gaming. The VFW was founded to advocate for those who serve in the military, veterans of the military, and their families. In Virginia, we seek to insure state-level laws and regulations provide care and support for those who have been in the military. The VFW conducts charitable gaming operations in accordance with state laws managed by various offices, but primarily those managed by the Office of Charitable and Regulatory Programs. The VFW is a fraternal, non-profit organization registered with the IRS as a 501c19 that is allowed to conduct charitable gaming in the Commonwealth. None of the work we do is partisan no campaign donations come directly from the VFW.
