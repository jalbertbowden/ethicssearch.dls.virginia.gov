  • EXECUTIVE AND LEGISLATIVE ACTIONS AND PROCUREMENT TRANSACTIONS

    Senate Bill 1172: Fare Enforcement Officer Currently Hampton Roads Transit has fare enforcement officers under ordinance governed by the City of Norfolk. The fine currently for The Tide operating in Norfolk is up to $250 if found in violation. Any expansion of public transportation services requiring a fare enforcement officer will need a new individual city ordinance from the city wanting the service. However, this bill will support a fare enforcement officer in place each of the six cities we service. This bill passed out of Senate with 30 Senators voting in favor of the bill. New Update on House Bill 1931 : Fare Enforcement Officer Yesterday, Senator Lucas made an amendment to the original House Bill 1931, which passed. The original bill only applied to planning districts in Richmond and Northern Virginia, which prevented us from supporting this bill. However, with Senator Lucas new amendment it would include the Hampton Roads region. Senate Bill 1456: Floor to Gas Tax (Hampton Roads Region) The Floor to the gas tax passed out of the senate with 26 voting in favor. This is a sign that we will have a hill to climb in the house with this legislation and each of you will be needed to call legislators to explain how important it is we create this gas floor identical to the state. House Bill 2136 : Washington Metrorail Safety Commission This bill has passed out of the house. Hampton Roads Transit is a firm supporter of Safety on all public transportation. By enacting this bill, the securing of federal funds would be solidified for transit agencies across the commonwealth of Virginia.
